What is Private Browsing Mode?

Private Browsing mode (sometimes called “Incognito Mode”) is a feature of modern web browsers that allows you to browse the web without leaving any trace of your activity on your computer. It's a separate window in your web browser where you're not logged into any of your websites. It's as if it's a fresh computer, every time you open it! When you close that window, all of your history and cookies from that browsing is immediately deleted. This is great for things like shopping for gifts, quickly checking on another email account, or when using a public computer.
